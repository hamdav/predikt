<p>Features:</p>
<ul>
<li><strong>Simple data entry</strong>: Type the (temperature / percentage / pH-level / other number) and just press &quot;Add&quot; to log the data now. If you want to log it at a different time, long press on &quot;Add&quot;.</li>
<li><strong>Sophisticated prediction</strong>: Uses a MCMC ensamble sampler to extrapolate the data. This automatically handles errors in the data and provides the most likely prediction, including a 95% confidence interval.</li>
<li><strong>Saving data series</strong>: If your data series is long, you can save it under a name and come back to it at a later date.</li>
</ul>
